Education

He started his early education at DAV Jawahar Vidya Mandir, Shyamali, Ranchi, Jharkhand where he was initially excelled at football & badminton.
He was goalkeeper of football team & was sent by his coach to play local cricket club. After his 10th standard, he focused on cricket. In 2008, after playing cricket for a long time, MS started his graduation & took admission at the St. Xavier’s College under sport quota.
Due to his busy cricket schedule, he was exempted from attending college classes. MS took first semester exams at his Ranchi home & passed them. Due to his busy cricket schedule, he was not able to give the second semester exams. The college did not allow him to sit into second year until & unless he gives the second semester exams.
